Tips for Breathing

• Choose a quiet, comfortable space for calm breathing.

• Sit or lie down with relaxed yet proper posture.

• Breathe slowly and deeply through your nose.

• Breathe through your stomach, not your chest.

• Exhale slowly, allowing your abdomen to contract.

• Focus on your breath, stay present, and relax.

• Count or visualize to enhance calm breathing practice.

• Calm breathing reduces stress and promotes relaxation.

• Practice calm breathing for a few minutes each day.

Benefits of Better Breathing

• Balanced levels of oxygen & carbon dioxide in the blood.

• Reduced levels of stress hormones in the blood.

• Increased physical energy & improved immune system.

• Increased feelings of calm and wellbeing.
